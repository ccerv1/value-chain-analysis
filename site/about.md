# About This Course

This site is the written companion to a guest lecture on Value Chain Analysis taught at Columbia SIPA since 2017 alongside Professor Glenn Denning.

## Speaker bio

[Carl Cervone](https://cerv.one) came to coffee through development work. After studying environmental science, he moved to Tanzania in 2004 to work in microfinance. In 2006, he joined [TechnoServe](https://www.technoserve.org/) and spent a decade working on coffee value chains across East Africa and Latin America. In 2016, he cofounded [Enveritas](https://www.enveritas.org/), which uses satellite data and machine learning to map and verify sustainability across coffee supply chains. In 2023, he cofounded [Kariba Labs](https://www.kariba.network/), which applies similar techniques to software value chains. He is a Columbia Business School alum.

The examples in this course draw from his work in coffee, international business, and data science.
